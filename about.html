About EcoGlow Candles

Our Story & Our Mission

Who We Are

EcoGlow Candles is a sustainable candle company dedicated to creating eco-friendly, hand-poured candles made from 100% natural soy wax. We believe in providing customers with beautiful, high-quality candles while caring for the environment.

Founded on the principle that luxury doesn't have to come at the cost of our planet, EcoGlow combines traditional candle-making craftsmanship with modern sustainability practices.

Our Mission

EcoGlow Candles serve to provide ambiance and relaxation while promoting sustainability. They are perfect for environmentally-conscious consumers looking for high-quality, long-lasting candles that leave a minimal carbon footprint.

We're on a mission to prove that eco-friendly products don't require compromises on quality, elegance, or performance.

Why Natural Soy Wax?

Soy wax is derived from soybean oil, a renewable resource that's biodegradable and completely natural. Unlike paraffin wax, soy wax:

  • Burns cleaner and longer
  • Produces less soot
  • Is fully renewable and biodegradable
  • Holds color and fragrance beautifully
  • Supports sustainable agriculture
